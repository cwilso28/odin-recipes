Oven Roasted Eggplant

Description

This recipe will tell you how to roast eggplants to perfection. This quick recipe is perfect for a week night meal and only takes 40 minutes to make! This recipe will make the perfect side.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds eggplant, chopped into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Place the eggplant on the baking sheet and toss with the olive oil and salt. Spread evenly on the baking sheet and roast for 25 to 35 minutes, or until tender and brown around the edges.
